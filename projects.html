MoodNail Picker

"MoodNail Picker" is designed for those like myself who passionately embrace the world of nail art and self-expression but find the decision-making process challenging. This project utilizes user input and colour data to recommend nail colors, eliminating the hassle of indecision. Users simply provide information about their current mood, the weather at their location, and the upcoming occasion and MoodNail Picker handles the rest to make the nail color selection process simple.
